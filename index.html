    Welcome to EveryBounty

    This is a Minecraft plugin meant to better the experiences of any faction or PvP based server by automatically creating and raising a bounty on every player to join a server, leading to a stronger player connection and more entertainment for all.

    EveryBounty is a work in progress plugin and there will certainly be errors.  
Any recommendations or help would be well appreciated :)

    Basics

    EveryBounty is designed to be a self-operating bounty plugin. It automatically creates a bounty editable in config and for a certain amount of time spent by a player online, raises the bounty a certain amount. It is meant to be a new edge for basic PvP or Faction servers that are looking for a new challenge between players.
